## The Challenge

WeLineo, a comprehensive franchise management platform, needed to centralize its internal operations. Relying on external, fragmented tools for team communication and employee onboarding was causing information silos, security concerns, and inconsistent training standards across different franchise locations and corporate environments.

## The Solution

I focused on architecting and developing two critical modules for the WeLineo PWA, built with React and Capacitor. First, I engineered a robust, WhatsApp-style real-time messaging module to handle secure, instantaneous communication between branch members and the corporate team. Second, I developed the Employee Training module, an interactive e-learning tool that allowed staff to seamlessly consume courses and training materials created by administrators. This required deep optimization of React's rendering cycle to handle live chat updates and multimedia content without degrading mobile performance.

## The Impact

By integrating these features natively into the WeLineo ecosystem, we successfully eliminated the dependency on third-party communication apps. The real-time messaging module drastically improved internal response times and team cohesion, while the training module standardized the onboarding process across all franchises, ensuring every employee had immediate access to critical corporate knowledge within a single, highly performant application.
