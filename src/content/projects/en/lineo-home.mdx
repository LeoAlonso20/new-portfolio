## The Challenge

Franchise administrators and corporate managers needed a centralized command center to oversee daily operations, human resources, and internal communications. Traditional corporate tools were dry and fragmented, leading to low user engagement. Furthermore, managing employee attendance, shifts, and training across multiple branches using disconnected systems was highly inefficient and prone to administrative bottlenecks.

## The Solution

I spearheaded the development of critical core modules for WeLineo Web, the administrative counterpart to the WeLineo App. To modernize internal communications and boost engagement, I architected a "Novedades" (Newsfeed) module functioning like an internal social network, complete with support for publishing photos, videos, and reels. Alongside a real-time messaging system, I engineered a robust HR attendance management module to track vacations, days off, and shifts. Finally, I built a comprehensive E-Learning module that empowers administrators to create full training courses, featuring multimedia sections, document handling, and custom evaluation grading systems.

## The Impact

The social and dynamic approach to internal communication drastically boosted user engagement and platform adoption across the company. Administrators gained a powerful, unified dashboard that drastically reduced HR administrative overhead and standardized corporate training delivery. By combining social interaction with rigorous management tools, the platform was successfully transformed from a basic utility into a highly engaging enterprise ecosystem.
