## The Challenge

The core biometric identity validation system was experiencing a nearly daily frequency of critical registration errors. This bottleneck was severely impacting onboarding success rates, causing friction for new users, and compromising the overall reliability of the platform.

## The Solution

I led a complete redesign and deep refactoring of the identity validation flow. To modernize the authentication experience, I implemented a seamless, passwordless login system utilizing the WebAuthn API for native biometric authentication. This was paired with an invisible Captcha integration to ensure robust security without degrading the user experience.

## The Impact

The re-engineering effort yielded immediate, measurable results. We successfully reduced the critical error rate to near 0%, drastically improving onboarding conversion rates and system stability. The new passwordless flow delivered a frictionless, highly secure authentication experience that met modern industry standards.
